About LittleExplorers

At LittleExplorers, we believe that learning begins with curiosity and wonder. Our platform was created in 2025 by akhilnath (first-year undergraduate students) as part of the Foundation of Computing and Web Development course.

We believe that every child deserves quality educational resources that are both entertaining and enriching. Through interactive games, enchanting stories, and creative activities, we help children develop crucial skills during their formative years.
